About Khatu Waada

Heritage, hospitality & Konkan flavours

Khatu Waada preserves the cultural heritage of Maharashtra while offering luxurious accommodations. Each room reflects local craft and design — wooden jharokhas, brass accents and hand-block prints. Step out to enjoy nearby historic sites, nature trails and river views.
