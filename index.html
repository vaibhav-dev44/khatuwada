About Our Heritage Home

Nestled in the scenic landscapes of Chiplun and Guhagar, Khatu Waada - The Heritage Home offers a unique blend of traditional architecture and modern comfort. Our resort preserves the rich cultural heritage of Maharashtra while providing luxurious accommodations.

Each room is designed to reflect local heritage with traditional decor, private balconies, and stunning views of the surrounding hills and rivers. Enjoy authentic Maharashtrian cuisine, cultural performances, and proximity to historical sites.

Whether you're seeking a peaceful retreat or an adventurous getaway, Khatu Waada is the perfect destination for families, couples, and groups.
